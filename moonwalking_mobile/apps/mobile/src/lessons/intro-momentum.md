
# Reading Momentum Tables

**Goal:** Quickly scan the 1m / 3m tables to spot reliable moves.

**Checklist** ✅
- Look for **px streak ≥ 3** (persistence) _and_ **1m% > 0.8**.
- Cross-check the **3m%**: sustained > burst.
- Avoid low integrity candles: **body ratio < 0.35**.

**Pro tip:** combine with **Volume Z** — a rising Z confirms real participation.
